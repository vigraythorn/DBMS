Granville Hotel

The Granville Sky Tower is located in one of the most beautiful coastline scenery destination of Dubai, the United Arab Emirates. You can have all-around ornamental ocean view and enjoy a stretch of nearly 700 metres of private white sand beach. And the Great Castle Hotel is located in a luxurious landscaped gardens, offering an experience of living in medieval environment for travelers.
